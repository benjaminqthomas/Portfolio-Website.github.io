Finlandia Infographic

Overview

Designing an Elegant, Informative Infographic for One of Finland’s Prized Vodkas

This infographic was created as a part of a comprehensive campaign for Finlandia Vodka. The goal was to capture some of the most influential information about Finlandia’s brand and display it in a visually consistent yet elegant way that guides the viewer through the infographic. Utilizing Finlnadia’s prized bottle and imagery of Finland which represents the brand's proud heritage, I was able to create a compelling and informative infographic that showcased who the brand is and its story.
