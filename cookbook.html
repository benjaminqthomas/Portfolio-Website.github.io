Mom's Cookbook

Overview

Designing and Printing a Delicious, Personal Cookbook in Honor of my Mother

This cookbook was created for my mother and our family inspired by some of the delicious meals she has made raising my siblings and me. My objective was to create a cookbook that commemorates my mother and feels somewhat rustic and gritty like a kitchen environment while also having a playful nature to it. Using textural overlays, unique, sharp, and round type pairings, and stylized, illustrative imagery, I was able to achieve my objective.
